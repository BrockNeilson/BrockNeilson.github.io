What is Hack Provo?

Hack Provo is a casual weekly collaborative meet-up where progress is made. We come together to meet, work and interact every Tuesday from 7pm - 10pm . Bring your own project or come and help someone in the community make progress and take their first steps!

All About Community

HackProvo will update the blog with projects that people are currently working on, news from our small community and tips and tricks for your side projects!

Collaboration

Meet people that as passionate as you are. Get advice, mentorship and maybe you'll find someone to help with your next project.

Progress

Get the ball rolling and make real progress on whatever it is you want to biuld!

Community

You can come just to help if you want. Teach, learn, collaborate, and help us build the Provo Community.
